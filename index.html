Pepe was a bored mexican farmer. Nothing special ever happened in his village. But one day, a strange phenomenon occurred. Chickens began to descend upon the village from all sides. Pepe knew that this was no ordinary chicken invasion and that he needed to act fast. Pepe quickly assessed the situation and realized that the chickens were being controlled by a powerful and evil force. He set out to find the source of this evil and put an end to it once and for all. As he battled his way through the endless hordes of chickens, Pepe could feel his strength waning. The chickens were relentless, and he knew that he would need to find a way to turn the tide of battle.
